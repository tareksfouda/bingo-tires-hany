Oil Changes: Keep Your Car Operating in Peak Condition
Ignore your engine’s oil at your own peril, because the potential problems (engine failure, corrosion, etc.) are real — and costly. What does clean oil do for your vehicle? A regular oil change:

1. Extends Your Engine’s Life - Heat and friction are in a constant battle with your engine and its oil. If unchecked, these two elements can lead down the long road to engine failure. Routine oil changes every 3,000 miles, or as recommended by your vehicle’s manufacturer, will sustain your engine for the long haul.

2. Eliminates All Contaminants - Dust. Dirt. Sludge — they’re all enemies of premier automotive performance. An oil change in North Carolina or Virginia done by the specialists at Bingo Tire removes contaminants by replacing the filter and using clean oil.

3.Improves Gas Mileage - An engine and its parts have to work in unison to maximize efficiency. Clean oil means no sludge or contamination, which translates to improvements in gas mileage and more.

Oil is the lifeblood of your engine. Entrust its management to the professionals. Bingo Tire performs oil, lube and filter changes throughout at our Virginia locations.
